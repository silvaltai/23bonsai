Common Questions

Everything you need to know about Rasgo.

Rasgo uses advanced AI models to generate hyper-realistic human images, videos, and consistent AI influencers. Simply enter a text prompt or upload a reference image, and Rasgo handles the rest, from generation to upscaling to video creation.

You can generate AI influencer photos, create videos with natural lip-sync and movement, upscale images up to 8K, place products into influencer scenes, and run full product photoshoots, all from one platform.

Most images generate in under 15 seconds. Video generation and upscaling times vary depending on resolution and complexity, but are typically completed within a minute.

Rasgo supports PNG, JPEG, and WebP for images, and MP4 for video. The upscaler can output images up to 8K resolution (7680 x 4320px), ready for print and production use.
